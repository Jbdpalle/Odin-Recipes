Hyderabadi Haleem

Description

Haleem is a type of stew that is widely consumed in the Indian subcontinent, the Middle East and Central Asia.

This dish varies from region to region, It is widely poplular in Hyderabad during Ramzan.

Ingredients

Haleem is made of 4 main components

  • Meat
  • Grain
    • Barley
    • Wheat
    • Pulses
  • Spices
  • Cooking liquid
    • Water
    • Milk
    • Broth

Procedure

This dish is slow cooked for seven to eight hours and then vigorously stirred or beaten with a pestle-like stirring stick. This results in a paste-like consistency, blending the flavors of spices, meat, barley and wheat.
